## Getting Started

1. **Access the System**
   - Open your web browser and navigate to the BAMS URL
   - Log in with your credentials

2. **Dashboard Overview**
   - View quick stats and recent activities
   - Access different modules from the sidebar

## User Roles

### Admin
- Manage users and permissions
- Configure system settings
- Generate system reports

### Teacher
- Mark student attendance
- View class reports
- Manage class schedules

### Student
- View personal attendance
- Check attendance history
- Download attendance certificates

## Marking Attendance

1. Navigate to the "Attendance" section
2. Select the class and date
3. Mark students as Present/Absent/Late
4. Click "Submit" to save (transaction will be recorded on blockchain)

## Viewing Reports

### Individual Reports
1. Go to "My Attendance"
2. Select date range
3. View or download the report

### Class Reports (Teachers/Admins)
1. Navigate to "Reports"
2. Select class and date range
3. Generate and export reports

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **Login Issues**: Ensure your credentials are correct and your account is active
- **Slow Performance**: Clear browser cache or try a different browser
- **Missing Data**: Check your internet connection and refresh the page
